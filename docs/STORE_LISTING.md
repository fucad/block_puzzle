**Long description:**

> The block puzzle you know — with everything you hate removed.
>
> Drag pieces onto the 8×8 board. Fill rows or columns to clear them.
> Chain clears into combos, empty the board for a huge bonus, and chase
> your best score.
>
> ★ CLASSIC — endless high-score play
> ★ QUEST — hand-crafted levels: score targets and gem-collecting
>   stages, with new level packs added regularly (no update needed)
>
> And what's NOT in the game:
> • No ads. Not even "optional" ones.
> • No in-app purchases. Nothing is locked.
> • No analytics, no tracking, no account.
> • The only network access is downloading new levels from our public
>   GitHub repository. Fully playable offline.
>
> Block Puzzle is free forever and open source (MIT). It's built and
> maintained by a community that believes simple games should be a
> public good. Play it, inspect it, fork it, or contribute a level pack:
> https://github.com/fucad/block_puzzle

**Category:** Puzzle
**Content rating:** Everyone
**Privacy policy summary:** The app collects nothing. There is no
analytics SDK, no advertising SDK, no account system, and no server of
ours. Quest content is fetched anonymously from GitHub's CDN; GitHub's
standard server logs apply to that request as with any website visit.
